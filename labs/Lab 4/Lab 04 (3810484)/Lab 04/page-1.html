DATE World War 2 Major Event
September 1, 1939 WW2 Starts by the invasion of Poland by Nazi Germany
June 4, 1940 Dunkirk evacuation, the British evacutes its defeated army
June 14,1940 Paris falls to the Nazi German invasion, and France soon follows
June 22,1941 Nazi Germany launches "Operation barbarossa", signaling the invasion of the Soviet Union
Note:
The soviets were caught off-gaurd due to the fact that Stalin was planning to invade Germany.
December 7, 1941 The United States enters the conflict after Japanese attack on Pearl Harbor
October,25 1942 Allied victory at Alamein marks the end of the Axis North Afrika campaign
February 1943 The Soviet Red Army recaptured the city of Stalingrad
June 6, 1944 The Allies launch Operation Overlord, the invasion of normandy beaches in Occupied France
May 8 1945 Germany loses and offically surrenders,End of the second world war in the europian theater. the date will be known as VE Day (Victory Europe)
